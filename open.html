How to open a library?

There are many steps in trying to open a library in your community. First Step should be

  1. Talk to community members about what they would want from a library
  2. Find a area where a library can be built or can be brought
  4. Find donations for books and other materials
  5. Hire qualified librarians
  6. Decide what type of event you can hold
